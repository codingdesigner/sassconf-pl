Scholastic Auditorium

557 Broadway, New York, NY 10012

Nestled in the heart of SoHo. Convenient access to all of New York City.

Scholastic Auditorium is located near the 7 train lines: Prince St. N, R, Broadway/Lafayette B,D,F,M, and the Spring St. 6. Stay in SoHo, Nolita, NoHO, West Village, Greenwich, or Tribeca to be a short walk away. Or explore nearby neighborhoods just a short train or Citi Bike ride away.
